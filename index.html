Integrating AI and Cybersecurity in Powerline Communication Systems

Cybersecurity Artificial Intelligence Powerline Communication Smart Grids Post-Quantum Cryptography

Powerline Communication (PLC) is a key technology in smart grids and IoT systems due to its cost efficiency and widespread availability. This paper examines the cybersecurity challenges introduced by integrating AI and cloud-based architectures into PLC networks. It analyzes vulnerabilities related to electromagnetic interference, physical access, and cloud dependency, and evaluates both traditional and AI-driven security mechanisms. The study further explores adaptive AI-powered anomaly detection and post-quantum cryptography as essential components for securing next-generation PLC systems deployed in critical infrastructure environments.

AI-Driven Medical Diagnostics: Advancing Early Disease Detection Through Intelligent Imaging

Artificial Intelligence Medical Imaging Computer Vision Deep Learning Healthcare Informatics Ethical AI

This paper explores the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in medical diagnostics, with a focus on early disease detection using computer vision and AI-assisted medical imaging. It contrasts traditional single-modality diagnostic systems, such as MRI or CT-based approaches, with emerging multi-modal imaging frameworks that provide a more comprehensive clinical view. The study examines AI-assisted imaging methodologies, including data preprocessing, image enhancement, and feature extraction, emphasizing the role of deep learning models—particularly convolutional neural networks (CNNs)—in medical image analysis. Additionally, it discusses domain-specific applications such as renal disease diagnosis, along with key challenges related to image quality, model performance, clinical integration, and the need for standardized and ethical AI validation frameworks. The findings highlight AI’s potential to enhance diagnostic accuracy, streamline healthcare workflows, and support more personalized and equitable patient care.
